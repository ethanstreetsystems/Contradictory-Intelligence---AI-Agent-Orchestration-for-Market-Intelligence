Contradictory Intelligence
AI Agent Orchestration for Market Intelligence
Overview
Contradictory Intelligence is a market intelligence platform that demonstrates how AI agents can automate large-scale financial research.
The system ingests high volumes of financial content including newsletters, podcast transcripts, and research commentary. AI agents process the information, extract structured insights, detect emerging narratives, and generate market intelligence outputs.
The platform functions as a lightweight DIY Bloomberg-style terminal designed to show how AI orchestration can transform research-heavy workflows.
Website: ethanstreet.com

The Problem
Financial market research remains highly manual.
Analysts read newsletters, listen to podcasts, and attempt to synthesize insights across hundreds of sources. This process is slow, expensive, and limited by human bandwidth.
Several structural problems emerge:
• Analysts cannot ingest information at scale • Signals are often buried across many sources • Narrative shifts may go unnoticed until they are widely recognized • Many organizations lack the infrastructure to automate research workflows with AI
As AI models become more capable, a new opportunity emerges: systems that continuously ingest information and transform unstructured content into structured intelligence.

Solution
Contradictory Intelligence demonstrates how AI agents can automate market research workflows.
The platform performs four core functions:
Data Ingestion Automated collection of newsletters, podcast transcripts, and research commentary.
AI Processing Large language models extract themes, companies, sectors, and narrative signals.
Intelligence Organization Insights are stored as structured data for trend analysis and cross-source comparison.
Automated Outputs The system generates market intelligence reports and a weekly market brief.
The result is a continuously updating intelligence system capable of processing far more information than a human analyst could realistically consume.
